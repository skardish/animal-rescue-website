About Us

Sit with me dog rescue believes in giving all dogs a second chance. We take in rescue dogs from all over Ontario and we do not discriminate, all dogs are welcome! We have worked with a wide variety of different dogs and with your help we have found over 300 dogs a permanent home to live!
